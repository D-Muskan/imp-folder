About Company

A home is a symbol of aspirations, and our intention is to enhance it with durable coatings that withstand the passage of time.

Milesaway is a range of luxury wood finishes from Grasim Industries Ltd (Birla Paints Div.) which is part of the Artmilesaway, a global conglomerate, in the league of Fortune 500 companies. The Milesaway range of wood finishes incorporates the latest Italian technology and would strive to bring consumers the highest standards of quality and finesse.
